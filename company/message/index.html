About us

Message from the president

Desire to contribute to
better world creation.

AR Brown group is in operations for more than 70 years as a technology trading company having its roots in the UK serving Electronics, Automotive, Chemical, Aviation & Aerospace, Pharmaceutical, Bio, and Medical related Industries. Recently we extended operations to Veterinary Industry in conjunction with geographical expansion to China, Thailand, India, and South Korea as entities, then Singapore, and Vietnam with salesforce deployment.

The Management vision “by coupling different professionalisms, deliver new values to the market and contribute to better all around the world creation” is meant that AR Brown group shall enhance professionalisms and dignity so that we can better collaborate with suppliers and partners to provide customers with credible solutions and highest level of services. Although rising geopolitical risks etc. makes it difficult to predicate world economies impacting even social environment, we are committed to making every effort with the vision in our mind. I look forward to your continuous guidance and encouragement.

April 2023

President
AR Brown Co., Ltd.
