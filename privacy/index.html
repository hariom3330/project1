Privacy policy

AR BROWN Web Site Online Privacy Policy

  1. Personal information
    Personal information provided to AR BROWN is information about a customer as an individual or company, which is identifiable to that specific individual or company, by the name, e-mail address, other description and/or individually assigned number, symbols, or codes.
  2. Management system
    AR BROWN assigns a team/personal in charge of managing personal information collected from the customers. AR BROWN provides an exclusive customer service window for this purpose, and the contact address will be clearly indicated in our Web Site.
  3. Collection of personal information
    Upon collecting personal information for the purpose of providing our service, AR BROWN will identify the purpose, and collect within a reasonable range. If a customer does not wish to provide personal information, he/she may choose to decline. However, the customer may not be able to obtain some of AR BROWN services that require certain information.
  4. Use of personal information
    AR BROWN uses and/or retains the collected personal information for the purpose clearly indicated to the customer. Unless otherwise agreed by the customer, the information will be used only for the purpose indicated to the customer.
  5. Disclosure of personal information
    AR BROWN will not disclose, or supply personal information to the third parties without the customer's own approval.
  6. Access and Correction of Personal Information
    Please contact us if you wish to verify or correct your personal information. To protect your security, only after we verify your identity, we will promptly proceed to take appropriate measures within a reasonable timeframe.
  7. Updates and improvements
    From time to time, AR BROWN may review and update this privacy policy.

Cookies

AR BROWN uses a software technology called "cookies" in some pages of our Web Site. Cookies are standard technology that the Web server identifies the customer's computer. Although cookies can identify your computer, it does not mean that your personal information is identifiable. If you do not wish to receive cookies in your computer, you can block them by the browser setting.

Security

When we ask for your personal information through our Web Site, we will use security technology such as the Secure Socket Layer protocol (SSL) if necessary, provided that your browser supports such technology, which automatically encrypts your name, address, phone number, and/or other information while being transmitted over the Internet.
