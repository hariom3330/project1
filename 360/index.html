ROOTSBorn in the United Kingdom growing up in Asia.

AR Brown’s roots is A.R Brown McFarlane & Co Ltd which was founded in 1889 by Albert Richard Brown (Captain Brown) in his hometown of Glasgow, Scotland. Captain Brown began construction of a lighthouse as an engineer of the Meiji Government, imported numerous ships such as the famous Meiji Maru (see *) from the United Kingdom and the United States, and contributed to the establishment of present-day Tokyo University of Marine Science and Technology. In 1885 he was inaugurated as the first General Manager of Nippon Yusen Kaisha Line, and in 1909, he was the first foreigner to receive the Second-Class Order of the Sacred Treasure from the Emperor Meiji awarding his contributions to the Wealth and Military Strength policy of the Meiji Era via enhancing Japan’s shipping capabilities.

The Tokyo Branch of A.R. Brown McFarlane & Co Ltd., which was founded in the Ginza District of Tokyo by Captain Brown’s grandson, Richard Pine Brown, during the post-war turmoil of 1949, 60 years after Captain Brown founded A.R. Brown McFarlane in Glasgow, is the present-day AR Brown Co., Ltd.

70TH
HISTORY

CHAPTER 01 1949-1969

From Foundation to Postwar Reconstruction:
Establishment Period of Technical Specialized Trading Company

When the Tokyo branch of AR Brown McFarlane was established, Dow Corning's silicone was first imported in 1950, British Atomic Energy Corporation's Isotope was imported in 1953 and United States mill standard compliant vibration testing equipment was imported to Japan in 1959 for the first time during strict foreign currency regulations. The company played an important role in the postwar Japanese economic recovery by contributing to improving the quality of Made in Japan products.

CHAPTER 02 1970-1986

Post Establishment of Bokusui Brown:
Management Expansion Period

The Tokyo branch of AR Brown McFarlane was closed and Bokusui Brown Co., Ltd was established by investment in Bokusui Brown Co., Ltd. The company began the sale of laboratory instruments, expanded the specialized chemical products it handled such as resin raw materials and catalysts, and contributed to the technological progress of the Japanese economy during the period of high economic growth.

CHAPTER 03 1987-2005

Independence from Bokusui:
Establishment Period of Independent AR Brown Management

AR Brown became independent from Bokusui Co., Ltd. in 1987. While the deflationary recession continued after the collapse of the bubble economy, AR Brown continued building the foundations of its independence, introduced new biotechnology and nanotechnology, and contributed to the evolution of genetic engineering and improvements in medical technology. In 1995, the company name was changed to AR Brown Co., Ltd.

CHAPTER 04 2006-2015

Focus on Management Philosophy and Motivation:
Management Transformation Period

A new management philosophy and vision for 2007 was set. The source of the company’s growth was determined to be in the growth of "People", and a workshop for the permeation of this philosophy was initiated giving top priority to the motivation of employees. During this time, financial reforms were realized after overcoming the Lehman shock, and in 2011, a shift to consolidated management was conducted by the establishment of overseas corporations in China in 2011 and Thailand in 2014.

CHAPTER 05 2016-2020

Focus on AR Brown in Asia by additional investment:
Corporate Transformation Period

A new management vision was established in 2016 with the advocation of "Brown in Asia", and corporations were established in India in 2018 and South Korea in 2019 with the goal of realizing a prosperous Asian region by the introduction of leading-edge technologies that combine the company’s expertise. “Investment” was added to "People" as a source of the company’s growth leading to AR Brown beginning to draw a new future image in Asia.

AR BROWN GROUP IN ASIA

AR Brown group has 6 entities (Japan*2, China, Thailand, India, South Korea) established and deploy salesforces in Singapore and Vietnam as well to provide optimum solutions for each of the regional markets. Also, by identifying unique products specific to the country or region, AR Brown group is supplying such products to the global markets simultaneously. Our operating mechanism is that each entity can collaborate with the other entities understanding the characteristics of the country and leveraging strengths or benefits of the country so that AR Brown group can contribute effectively and efficiently to value creation of the customer. We hope to be a bridge between Asian countries and another bridge between Asia and the global market.

Japan

AR Brown Co., Ltd. and FB Automation Co., Ltd. exist. AR Brown Co., Ltd. has Tokyo HQs, Nagoya and Osaka offices, and Institute of Science and Technology.

China

Electronics Materials, Performance Chemical, Fine Chemical and Test & Measurement businesses are conducted for the China markets. For Performance Chemical and Fine Chemical businesses, it also has functionality to source products exporting to the other entities of the group. Branch office exists in Guangzhou.

Thailand, Singapore, Vietnam

Electronics Materials, Performance Chemical, Fine Chemical, Life Science and Test & Measurement businesses are conducted for the ASEAN markets. For Electronics Materials and Performance Chemical businesses, Thailand has functionality to source products exporting to the other entities of the group as well. Salesforces were deployed in Singapore and Vietnam respectively.

India

The entity started from sourcing of products for Performance Chemical and Fine Chemical businesses. As of now the entity conducts Electronics Materials, Performance Chemical, Fine Chemical, Hu-Vet and Test & Measurement businesses for the India markets. Furthermore, through investment into an Indian API and Intermediate manufacturer, contracted production capability for relevant products has been obtained.

South Korea

Electronics Material and Performance Chemical businesses are conducted.
