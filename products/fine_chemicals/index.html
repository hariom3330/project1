Specialty and Fine Chemicals

In this department, we are developing new markets with the latest technology and new performance materials.

We can offer Materials for adhesive, pharmaceutical, high performance resin, paint & ink and aerospace industries by making full use of our network of European and US suppliers, for example Kraton corporation world leader in adhesive and toll oil products, Day-Glo No.1 Fluorescent Pigments maker, Rhodia , Wychem, Norchem, RCA, Prom Chem, Aquaspersions, Japan Sun Oil and so on.

We, as the agent of those suppliers, supply the above products to customers with up-dated technical and market information.

Additionally, we are making every endeavor to comply with the much stricter quality and environmental regulations that are predicted for the near future.
