Simple procedure and get the result in 5 minutes

�� With 3 steps procedure, easy to use for the purpose to check drug residue in balk at farmer side too.

�� The result in 5 minutes and easy to get result by eyes interpretation

  • STEP 1

    • Take the milk sample with a Dropper up to the level of the indicated line, and dispense all amount of the milk into the plastic Cup.
    Do this operation Twice.

  • STEP 2

    • Jiggle the Plastic Cup lightly to spread the sample on the bottom surface of plastic cup

  • STEP 3

    • Soak the Dipping part of Test Strip in the milk sample of the plastic Cup.
    * Do not place the strip vertically
