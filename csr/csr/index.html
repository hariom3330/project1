Management Philosophy with Meaning

The AR Brown Group is a company that works as much as possible to obtain the trust of the international community by conducting trading company activities at the forefront of the times, and providing products and services that help customers in Japan and around the world create new value with our management philosophy in mind. In order to achieve this goal, the AR Brown Group has announced that the "AR Brown Group Corporate Ethics Action Policy" will be put into practice in order to increase management transparency, carry out fair and appropriate corporate activities, and fulfill the social responsibilities required by society. President

Compliance with Laws and Social Norms Respect for Human Rights and Work Environment/Health/Labor Management Environmentally Friendly Quality Management Fair Trade Information Security Crisis Management Relationship with stakeholders Sustainability

Compliance with Laws and Social Norms

The AR Brown Group recognizes that we are a member of the global society by respecting the culture and customs and complying with the laws and spirits of each country, and therefore acts with high ethical standards in order to promote sound business activities.

Respect for Human Rights and Work Environment/Health/Labor Management

The AR Brown Group supports the growth of individuals based on the belief that the success of our business activities is achieved by the development of our individuality and abilities. In this light, the AR Brown Group will respect the human rights and dignity of all people involved in our business activities, will not tolerate any discriminatory acts against any employee, and will provide fair working conditions. The AR Brown Group will work towards improving the working environment where all employees are able to play an active role in a safe and healthy workplace.

Environmentally Friendly

The AR Brown Group recognizes the need for environmental protection on a global scale. We comply with relevant laws and regulations in each country, and also protect the environment in life cycle of procurement, sales, usage, recycling, and disposal across all of our business activities. The AR Brown Group will strive to contribute to the creation of a sustainable society.
also work on the development and diffusion of technology that balances both the environment and the economy.

Quality Management

The AR Brown Group will comply with laws and norms related to quality standards such as safety in each country and region, and also strive to improve quality with the customer's first stance by giving priority to quality. We will establish a system to continue and promote company-wide quality control activities.

Fair Trade

We do not exclude free competition in transactions with our customers at the AR Brown Group, and fair trade is conducted in accordance with the laws, rules and customs of each country such as the Antimonopoly Act. We will respect our business partners as business partners, determine trade conditions such as prices, quality, and contracts fairly, and word towards building mutual trust. We will not give or receive excessive entertainment that deviates from the conventional wisdom to our business partners and civil servants, both domestically and internationally.

Information Security

We recognize the importance of confidential corporate information and personal information learned in the course of business both inside and outside the group, and we take appropriate security measures to manage and protect it. We will take protective measures against threats on computer networks and manage them so that they do not damage ours or other companies.

Crisis Management

In order to protect the life and physical safety of everyone concerned, the AR Brown Group will aim to determine the risks of natural disasters, infectious diseases, etc., and in the event of an emergency including changes in social conditions, the group will minimize the damage promptly. We will take recovery measures and provide appropriate information.

Relationship with stakeholders

The AR Brown Group will ensure that the duties carried out by each AR Brown Group company complies with laws and regulations, and we will work towards constructing an appropriate and efficient management system so that the trust of all stakeholders is earned. We shall improve management transparency by disclosing highly useful corporate information regarding management policies, business details and financial information in a timely and appropriate manner.

Sustainability

Based on our management vision of "contributing to the creation of a prosperous society," we will promote sustainability through social contribution activities, donations, and investments in addition to business activities which contribute to solving environmental and social problems. We will fulfill our and corporate social responsibilities.
