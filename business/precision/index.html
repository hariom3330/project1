Business Introduction

Performance Chemical

In 1960, AR Brown started dealing in fluorescent pigments and from 1980s Pine Chemical and in 1990s Biocides were added to the product offerings. In recent years, Adding Specialty Chemical products made in China, ASEAN and India, AR Brown serves Tire, Chemical Intermediates, Adhesive, Lubricant, Personal Care, Household Care, Pain & Ink, Aviation & Aerospace Industries in Asia by supplying specialty chemical products such as Bio-based chemicals.
