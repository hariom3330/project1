Business Introduction

Test & Measurement

AR Brown has been contributing to quality improvement in Automotive, Tire, Electronics, and Aviation & Aerospace Industries since starting to deal in MIL-standard vibration and impact testing machines in 1959. In 1996, AR Brown participated in logistics environment tests, and in 2009, started to deal in surface pressure mapping sensors. In 2013, AR Brown started to provide Automotive Industry in Japan with ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance System) testing systems, which are helping the Automotive Industry in Japan lead the safe driving technologies development globally and its field has extended to China Automotive Industry as well. In China AR Brown also deals in Medicine Packaging machines for hospitals and Strain Gauges for Automotive Industry. In 2022, FB Automation Co., Ltd. was acquired, who deals in liquid control devices such as precise valves and pumps for Medical and Analytical Equipment Industries.
